Jolt

Get up. Make your sound.

An alarm that only stops when your phone hears you do the first thing. Shake your pills, start the toothbrush, grind the coffee. There is no snooze and no button to mash. Everything runs on your phone.

Join the testers Read the story

How it works

  • 1Record a sound only you can make by getting up. Your pill bottle, an electric toothbrush, the coffee grinder.
  • 2Set your alarms. Morning, evening, whenever you need them.
  • 3Make the sound to stop it. It flashes and rings until the phone hears that exact sound. No sound, no silence.

Why it's different

  • No snooze to mash. The off switch is a real action you take with your body.
  • It listens for your specific sound, matched entirely on your phone.
  • A press-and-hold escape if you ever cannot, so you are never truly trapped.
  • It wakes you by sound, by screen flash, and by vibration, so it works even if you cannot see or hear.
  • No account. No ads. No tracking. Works offline. Free to start.
