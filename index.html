Rattle

Shake your pills to stop the alarm.

A medication alarm you can't silence by tapping snooze half-asleep — you stop it by physically shaking your real pill bottle. The off switch is the gesture that gets the pill in your hand.

Coming soon to Google Play

How it works

  • 1Record your bottle once. Rattle learns its rattle.
  • 2Set your alarms. Morning, evening, whenever.
  • 3Shake to stop. It flashes and rings until it hears that bottle. No shake, no silence.

Why it's different

  • No snooze button to mash — the off switch is a real-world action.
  • It listens for your bottle, matched entirely on your phone.
  • A gentle press-and-hold escape if you ever can't shake — never truly trapped.
  • No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Works offline. Free.
